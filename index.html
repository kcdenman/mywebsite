About Me

I first learned of Bitcoin in 2015 and quickly became obsessed with it. Nothing since the Internet has had more potential to fundamentally disrupt human civilization. As a millennial born in 1988, I didn't get to help build the Internet. Bitcoin represented my once-in-a-lifetime opportunity to make a real impact.

I have a systems engineering degree but I am not an engineer by trade. In the web3 space, I work on strategy, business development, and partnerships. My technical background designing and implementing large systems on multidisciplinary teams combined with my business development skills are my superpower. Don't let this fancy website trick you into thinking I can code. I can read but not write. AI wrote the HTML and CSS that brought this site to your eyes.
