Philosophy

Curiosity. I believe curiosity is one of the most important human characteristics. I think humans who are drawn to asking difficult questions and put the work in to

seeking out the true answers tend to be both happy and successful. Curiosity and humility are related in that someone who is very curious must have some degree of

humility.

Execution. I believe that execution is very important. It is really important to not just be an idea person. You need to implement things and see your ideas through.

This applies to little things and big things.

Decentralization. I believe in the power of atomic and distributed systems. This concept goes beyond technology for me. I think society, culture, food, politics, as well as

the Internet have all gotten too centralized and top heavy. I believe Bitcoin started us on a path towards a more decentralized form of humanity as it provided a

key primitives necessary to establish a sovereign network using inexpensive digital tools only. The book The Sovereign Individual by James Dale Davidson, Michael David Axtell

greatly helped me see this centralized-decentralized pendulum more clearly.
