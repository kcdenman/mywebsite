Education

I have a Bachelors of Science degree in Industrial Engineering (aka Systems Engineering) from West Virginia University.

Prior to transferring to the Engineering school, I spent a year as a Finance major learning about financial markets. As I began learning about the application of differential equations to design obscure credit default swaps it became clear to me that while Finance is critically important, my young brain might be safer in an Engineering school.

Today, with the rise of crypto, I am excited to draw from both my Engineering and Finance experience to contribute to the global system design of our increasingly fragile financial infrastructure.
